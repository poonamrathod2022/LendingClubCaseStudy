## General Information

- When the company receives a loan application, the decision for loan approval has to be made by the company, based on the applicant’s profile.
Two scenarios that are likely to occur –
case1 - Applicant likely to repay the loan. If loan not approved, Loss of business for the company
case2 - Applicant not likely to repay the loan ( "Risky applicants"). If loan approved, financial loss ( called credit loss) for the company

- The objective is to use EDA and provide insights which can be used by the company to make decisions on loan approval/rejection.

- The dataset used is this case study is loan.csv which is provided in the upgrad.
